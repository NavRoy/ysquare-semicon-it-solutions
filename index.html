ENGINEERING SERVICES

Engineering Solutions That Power Innovation

YSQUARE SEMICON IT SOLUTIONS delivers advanced engineering services across Semiconductor, Embedded Systems, Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Technologies, and Digital Transformation. We partner with startups, enterprises, and global organizations to build innovative, scalable, and future-ready technology solutions.

Semiconductor Engineering

End-to-end semiconductor design services including RTL Design, Functional Verification, Physical Design, DFT, STA, and ASIC implementation for next-generation silicon products.

Embedded Systems

Design and development of embedded firmware, device drivers, BSPs, RTOS applications, and IoT solutions for industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics.

Software Development

Custom web applications, enterprise software, mobile applications, cloud-native platforms, and scalable digital solutions tailored to your business goals.

Digital Engineering

AI-driven automation, machine learning, data analytics, computer vision, and intelligent solutions that transform business operations.
