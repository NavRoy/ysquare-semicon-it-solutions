Who We Are

Engineering Built
for What's Next

YSQUARE Semicon IT Solutions is a precision engineering company serving the global semiconductor, automotive and hi-tech industries. We exist to close the gap between complex engineering ambition and reliable, production-grade delivery.

Founded on the principle that domain depth beats generalist consulting, every engagement is staffed by specialists who understand what it means to ship hardware and software at scale — under schedule, under budget, and without compromising quality.

From silicon design to cloud-native software platforms, from embedded firmware to AI-powered automation — YSQUARE brings the engineering capability that modern product companies need to compete in a world where technology cycles are faster than ever.

Our Values

The Standards We
Hold Ourselves To

Five principles that govern how we engage, how we deliver, and what our clients can expect from every YSQUARE engineering team.

Integrity
We say what we mean, deliver what we commit to, and raise concerns early — without exception, regardless of commercial pressure.
Innovation
We actively explore better ways to engineer — from toolchains to methodologies — and bring that curiosity to every client engagement.
Quality
Zero defect is the baseline. Every deliverable — from a line of RTL to a production firmware stack — is reviewed against the highest engineering standard.
Collaboration
We operate as embedded extensions of your engineering team — sharing context, owning problems, and communicating with the transparency of internal colleagues.
Excellence
Not in mission statements. In every tape-out, every firmware release, every code review — excellence is the standard we're held to and the one we hold ourselves to.
