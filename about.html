Culture

A Culture Built
on Ownership

We don't hire generalists and hope they figure it out. Our culture is built around specialists who take personal ownership of outcomes — the same way they would on their own product.

Integrity
We say what we mean, deliver what we commit to, and raise concerns early — without exception, regardless of commercial pressure.
